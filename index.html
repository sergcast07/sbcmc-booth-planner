Click a box to select it — its handles stay visible and clickable until you select something else or click empty space, so you don't have to keep the mouse hovering just right. Drag to move it, or double-click to type a name (a vendor, a sponsor, anything) right on the box — it replaces the package letter and saves with the layout. The top knob drags to rotate freely (snaps to 15° steps, hold Shift for exact free angles); the dark ↻ badge jumps a quick 90°; the red × removes it. Each package's counter shows how many you've placed so far, added up across every map.

Drops a text tag on the current map. Double-click it to type anything — a room name, a note, an arrow callout. Drag to reposition; drag the bottom-right corner to resize (shows the size in feet as you drag); hover for the × to remove.
